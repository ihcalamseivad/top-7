Basketball, a sport invented by Dr. James Naismith in 1891, has evolved into a global phenomenon beloved by millions. Played on a rectangular court with hoops mounted at each end, basketball is characterized by its fast-paced action, dynamic teamwork, and electrifying displays of athleticism. Players aim to score points by shooting the ball through the opponent's hoop while simultaneously defending their own basket.
